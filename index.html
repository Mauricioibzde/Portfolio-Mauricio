About me

My journey into the world of IT started when I was 13 years old and had my first contact with computers on a technical course. Without a computer to practice on - because of financial struggles - I decided on a unique way to build one piece by piece, studying the hardware involved in depth. This experience not only taught me the importance of resourcefulness and perseverance but also sparked a deep interest in understanding and creating technology.

I then delved into operating systems and discovered my passion for design, exploring Adobe programs such as Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere Pro, After Effects, as well as CorelDraw. Throughout my studies, I faced challenges and problems, but by researching and solving them, I gained a lot of experience as a graphic designer.

Years later, I met a friend who introduced me to web programming, specifically JavaScript.
Although I wasn't interested at first, I needed to create a website for my other passion, dance.
After several failed attempts, I realized that I needed to study programming.

My interest was rekindled and I learned all the skills needed to get started as a front-end developer.

Thanks to my dancing talent, I was given the opportunity to come to Europe about 2 years ago and work in a French dance company.
This chance got me closer to my dream of furthering my education in IT.
I am very grateful for this and I would not want to miss this opportunity.
I cordially invite you to take a look around here!
